Editor scripting API

set(x, y, z, r, g, b)

Sets a voxel at the given coordinate with the given color components.

arguments:
x, y, z: The coordinate at which to place a voxel. Should be integer values.
r, g, b: The red, green, and blue color components. Should range from 0.0 to 1.0.

unset(x, y, z)

Unsets a voxel at the given coordinate.

arguments:
x, y, z: The coordinate of the voxel to remove. Should be integer values.

get(x, y, z)

Gets the voxel data at the given coordinate.

arguments:
x, y, z: The coordinate of the voxel whose information is desired.
returns:
x, y, z: the coordinate of the voxel
r, g, b: the color components of the voxel

clear()

Clears all voxels.

setCamera(angle, elevation, radius, x, y, z)

Sets the postion and orientation of the camera.

arguments:
angle: The angle the camera is rotated around the Y-axis. Same as left/right mouse motion while holding the left mouse button.
elevation: The angle the camera is elevated away from the XZ-plane. Same as up/down mouse motion while holding the left mouse button.
radius: How far from the focus the camera is. Same as mouse wheel.
x, y, z: The translation of the focus. Similar to up/down/left/right mouse motion while holding the right mouse button.
